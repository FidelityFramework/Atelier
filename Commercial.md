# Atelier Commercial License

## Overview

Atelier will be available under a Commercial License for use in commercial products, services, and enterprise environments. The Commercial License provides rights and protections beyond those offered by the Apache License 2.0, and enables access to Professional tier features.

## Atelier Editions

Atelier will be available in two editions:

### Community Edition (Apache 2.0)

The Community Edition is free and open source under the Apache License 2.0. It includes:

- Full editor functionality with CodeMirror 6
- Panel docking with Dockview
- Basic LSP integration via FSNAC
- PSG visualization
- Terminal integration
- Multi-WebView architecture

Suitable for:
- Individual developers
- Open source projects
- Educational use
- Evaluation and learning

### Professional Edition (Commercial License)

The Professional Edition requires a Commercial License and includes everything in Community plus:

- **Advanced Debugging**: Full delimited continuation inspector with visualization
- **Pipeline Inspector**: Complete compilation pipeline visibility (PSG, MLIR, LLVM IR, Assembly)
- **Effect System Tooling**: Effect annotations, mismatch diagnostics, and effect graphs
- **Performance Verification**: Integration with perf, VTune, and hardware performance counters
- **Cache Analysis**: False sharing detection and cache line visualization
- **Priority Support**: Direct access to the development team
- **Commercial Use**: Rights for commercial products and services

## Commercial License Benefits

The Commercial License for Atelier provides:

- **Professional Features**: Access to all Professional Edition capabilities

- **Expanded Usage Rights**: Full rights to use, modify, and distribute Atelier in commercial products and services

- **No Open Source Requirements**: Freedom from the obligation to open source your own code that integrates with Atelier

- **Legal Indemnification**: Protection against intellectual property claims related to your use of Atelier (available with Enterprise support tiers)

- **Priority Support**: Access to technical support, bug fixes, and security updates

- **Customization Options**: Ability to request custom features and modifications

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need a Commercial License?**

A: You need a Commercial License if you want access to Professional Edition features, if you are using Atelier in a commercial product or service, if you want commercial support, or if you need to distribute Atelier as part of a proprietary solution.

**Q: Can I try Atelier before purchasing a Commercial License?**

A: Yes, you can use the Community Edition under the Apache License 2.0 indefinitely. You can also request a trial of Professional Edition features before purchasing.

**Q: What happens when my Commercial License expires?**

A: You will need to renew your license to continue using Professional Edition features and receive support. You can continue using the Community Edition under the Apache License 2.0.

**Q: Can I upgrade from one license tier to another?**

A: Yes, you can upgrade your license tier at any time. The cost difference will be prorated based on the remaining term of your current license.

**Q: Is the source code available for Professional Edition features?**

A: Professional Edition features are included in the same repository. The licensing determines which features you have rights to use commercially.
